# Foundations of AI Ethics — Interactive Kiosk Design Ideas

## Selected Approach: Museum-Grade Dark Academic

<response>
<text>
**Design Movement**: Dark Academic / Museum Exhibition Design
**Core Principles**: 
1. Deep navy (#1a1f3d) background as the canvas — evoking gravitas and scholarly depth
2. Gold accents for connections, borders, and headings — signifying knowledge lineage
3. White/cream for body text — maximum readability against dark backgrounds
4. Touch-first interactions with generous hit targets for kiosk use

**Color Philosophy**: Navy represents the depth of philosophical thought; gold represents the precious thread of intellectual lineage connecting centuries of ethics; white represents clarity and truth.

**Layout Paradigm**: Three-tier vertical architecture — Classical Philosophers (top arc) → Core Principles (middle row of diamond nodes) → Modern Pioneers (bottom arc). Curved SVG connection lines flow between tiers. Portrait-optimized for vertical kiosk (1080x1920).

**Signature Elements**: 
1. Gold-bordered circular portrait frames with soft glow on interaction
2. Diamond-shaped principle nodes that pulse with golden light
3. Curved SVG connection paths that animate with flowing gold particles

**Interaction Philosophy**: Progressive disclosure — idle shows all connections pulsing; tap highlights one pathway; tap again expands key info; tap "Read More" for deep dive. 7-minute idle auto-reset.

**Animation**: Soft pulsing glow on idle connections, smooth opacity transitions when selecting pathways, slide-in panels for info cards, flowing particle effect along connection lines.

**Typography System**: Playfair Display for headings (serif, academic gravitas), Inter for body text (clean readability on screens). Gold headings, white body text.
